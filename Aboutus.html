ABOUT US

"Technology is a gift of God. After the gift of life it is perhaps the greatest of God's gifts. It is the mother of civilizations, of arts and of sciences."


Tech Talk is a small initiative taken by the students of UIT-RGPV to spread technology awareness among the students. We believe it is us the youth who can take technology awareness to the next level. We always come across people or seller who force their ideas related to technology like saying AMD and Intel are the same(Which are not). We want to make you independent so that when you go to buy new products its always easy for you to select the product. We want to make you aware of the latest technology. We want you to boast your tech knowledge to everyone you meet also We want you to promote tech knowledge awareness among the public. Tech Talk is a unified community talking about the latest and the greatest of all the big tech giants in the world of computer technology. We also talk about startups in this field.

We as a team of geeks keep ourselves updated with the latest technology to promote it. We go in colleges to give all tech knowledge we have, when we stand on the podium to talk about technology we tell every small bit about it and that too in a interactive way so that students can grasp it easily. We are driven by the urge to keep our India updated, our India is getting digital, so should we. After listening to us, student will have enough knowledge about the topic we spoke about. They will be able to stand up against people who have less or no knowledge. We also try to give a hands-on experience of any one technology that we covered in our seminars, like we organise ARA-Hunt to give a hands-on experience of Google X's Project ARA.

Finally, we would like to Say change begins from us. It is our job to spread knowledge. We are trying, so should you to make India digital.
