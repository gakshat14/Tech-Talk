"Technology is a gift of God. After the gift of life it is perhaps the greatest of God's gifts. It is the mother of civilizations, of arts and of sciences."

Tech Talk is a small initiative taken by the students of UIT-RGPV to spread technology awareness among the students. We believe it is us the youth which can take technology awareness to the next level, telling everyone about the latest tech. We always come accross people or seller who force their ideas related technology like saying AMD and Intel are same. We want to make you independent so that when you guys go to buy anything its always easy for you to select the product according. Unlike other tech clubs we are unified community talking about the latest and the greatest of all the big tech giants as well as about tech startup.
